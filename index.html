Our Products

Cake

Cake is our sole sensor in the traditional market segement. Released in 2013, this sensor has been in the market for 11 years and our research and development has helped it to continue to meet consumer needs. Cake was most recently updated in May 2024. It is currently taking on a 17% market share in the market segment and we sold out by year end of 2024.

Cedar

Cedar, released in 2012, is our only low-end sensor. It was recently re-released in 2024 to better suit consumer needs. The product is characterized by its low prices combined with technology that has been proven to be the best in the market for years.

Cid

Cid is the older model of our high-end sensor. Don't be mistaken; old, in this case, only means that it was released first. This sensor has been continually updating since its release in 2015 to meet the high standards and expectations of high-end consumers.









Coat

Coat is our only performance sensor in the market. It is part of market with few competitors and it has been that way since its release in 2014. Our performance sensor has reached up to 28% market share, proving that our products are highly desired by consumers. Coat has managed to achieve the highest customer accessibility within all the performance sensors in the market.

Cure

Cure is one of the 2 size market sensors that our company has released. This was released in 2014 and has undergone intense development every single year since. In 2024 alone, we sold over 900 units of Cure.

Cotton

Cotton is the newer of our company's 2 high-end sensors. This item was launched in 2020 after many years of development, in combination with feedback from consumers about Cid. This product is currently the market leader in customer survey score, meaning that the market demand for our product is the highest.




Candy

Candy is our newest release in the size market. It has had a significant presence in the market. For the year of 2024, it was the only sensor in the market that sold out completely. It is also currently at the highest performance out of all the sensors of size market.
